INDOOR NAVIGATION AND POSITIONING

PinPoint offers indoor navigation and indoor positioning. Our system uses Bluetooth beacons and AI based localization for accuracy. This allows our system to be used in different environments like airports, hospitals, and large complex structures easily and reliably.

INDOOR NAVIGATION SYSTEM

Accurate Indoor Navigation

Our indoor navigation solution provides accurate turn-by-turn navigation to users.
  • Turn-by-turn directions throughout designated navigation zones
  • Visual display guidance
  • Voice guidance
  • Audio prompts
  • Phone "Shake" alerts
  • Automatic rerouting
  • Integration with other applications
  • multiple route options

Navigation for Visually Impaired

Our system allows people with visually impairments independently explore new indoor environments.
  • 1~2m positioning accuracy
  • Visually impaired/Wheelchair mode
  • Textiles and floor changes alerts
  • Audio prompts of environment changes and hazards
  • audio prompts for facility information

Support

Clients can edit destinations and environmental changes and information on a live desktop.
  • Sensor network maintenance
  • Map server support
  • Customized add-ons
  • Trajectory statistics
  • Space utilization data

BENEFITS

  • Improving your service by getting people to their destinations without confusion

  • Helping facility decisions by viewing user's traffic patterns
  • Enhancing revenue through potential marketing and advertising capabilities
  • Integrating with existing facility systems to increase adoption and client/patient on-time arrivals
